To Someone Special

To someone,

Who notices the mood swings of an insane girl (mood thunders what he calls),

Who sit by her side whenever she gets down,

Who wake up in the midnight just to see her,

To make sure that she is doing good

Who put his best to make her happy...

Whenever she needs his care

He appears like an angel...

Who sits by her side and tell the stories that she wish to hear,

Who does all of those childish acts just to put a smile on her face...

Words are not enough to explain how much he mean to her

She is so obliged to have him in her life

As her best friend

As her boy friend

As her everything
